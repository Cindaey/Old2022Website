Mulberry Street Floristry (2021)

Mulberry Street Floristry website is a business site of a flower shop where people can shop for flowers that are suitable for different occasions online. Customers can obtain information related to different flowers available in the store from the website.

This project was a portfolio piece done during the time I was very new to the study of web

Highlights

Accessible Flower Preview Slide Show:

Customers may preview the flowers on the home page and navigate to the individual flower pages upon clicking the caption.

Individual Flower Overview:

Each flower has an individual page containing information about them, such as price, general information, care tips, etc. The pages also include additional navigation for flowers suitable for similar occasions in order to have a more efficient user flow.
