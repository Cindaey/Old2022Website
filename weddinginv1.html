Wedding Invitation Layout Design (2021)

This is a wedding invitation layout design consisting of the invitation card, save-the-date,rspv, and the reception card. The series has two variations with one being the dark version while the other being the light version.

The theme of this invitation package is a modern seaside wedding. Each of the items within the package has a different message that serves the purpose of that specific item. For the typeface, an elegant script typeface combined with a san serif typeface were chosen to suit the nature of this occasion. The reason behind this selection relates to the theme of the wedding being modern yet maintains its elegant style of it being a seaside wedding.
